Interactive Lessons

Chinese the Right Way uses interactive lessons and toys to make the learning experience more engaging and interesting for students.

Flashcards, dice, quizzes, and other activities are used to break up the monotany of memorizing characters by rote. Students can create accounts to track their learning process, bookmark activies for later, and request help from their peers and volunteer teachers.

Learning Through Tradition

Chinese the Right Way uses an emphasis on traditional learning strategies and storytelling in order to make the Chinese language fun and easy to learn.

Developed by Students

This webservice is developed by Cody Lee with the guidance of Mercer University's Dr. Martin Zhao. The website is provided "as is" to help other students develop their chinese skills.

Data is pooled from public sources such as HanziDB or Unihan to increase the amount of information provided. Other content is developed internally to supplement the learning experience.

Chinese the Right Way includes lessons and books written by Dr. Martin Zhao which will help new learners not only learn about the written and oral aspects of the Chinese language, but also enrich the student's understanding of Chinese culture.
